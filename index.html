About LioranBoard

Along with being a completely free stream deck, LioranBoard is a very powerful tool that lets your Twitch viewers control and interact with your stream. The software may seem a bit intimidating at first, but the GUI is quite accessible and there are many video guides on the web to get you started. In no time, you’ll make LioranBoard take control of basic things, such as changing scenes automatically or showing custom alerts. Beyond that, the sky is the limit: you can add several commands to the same button to achieve creative and original effects, or even run JavaScript extensions to connect to other apps and services. Learning how to work with LioranBoard is a bit like learning programming, and mastering it means you can make it do almost anything you can imagine.

LioranBoard is compatible with OBS Studio and has stream decks for both PC and Android.
