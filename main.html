About LioranBoard

LioranBoard is a very powerful streaming tool that lets your Twitch viewers control and interact with your stream.
It is a bit like a programming language where you can easily do a lot of basic things (like automatic scene changing and custom alerts), but also get extremely creative and program some pretty amazing stuff.
Lioranboard can be connected to other apps and services via JavaScript extensions, so there is really nothing stopping your creative mind!
LioranBoard is compatible with OBS Studio and most of all, it's completely free!
StreamDeck is available for both PC and Android.
