YouTube Live LioranBoard

YouTube Live is an extension for LioranBoard, which allows our users to go live directly from LioranBoard, listen to YouTube chat, send and manage chat messages and more.

By using our extension, you're allowing it to access and modify your Youtube account (i.e. schedule broadcasts, listen to YouTube chat events, send and delete YouTube chat messages). This is all done locally on your computer and is never shared with any third parties.

You can revoke the extension's access to your YouTube account at any time by following this link.
